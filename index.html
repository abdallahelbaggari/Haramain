🔒 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 17 May 2026 · Powered by Haramain.pi · Pi Network Testnet

1. Information We Collect

HARAMAIN collects the following information when you use the platform:

  • Pi Network username — provided by Pi Network upon authentication
  • Location data — only when you activate Qibla Compass, Prayer Times, or Live Tracker (with your explicit permission)
  • Health information — blood type, allergies, medications — stored locally on your device only, never transmitted to our servers
  • Usage data — worship activities (Tawaf, Dhikr), points, streak — stored locally on your device
  • Payment data — transaction IDs only, processed securely by Pi Network. We never store payment credentials.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide prayer times, Qibla direction, and weather alerts based on your location
  • Generate your Pilgrim ID and QR code for identification and emergency use
  • Process Pi Network payments for Pilgrim Pass, Zakat, Sadaqah, and Qurbani
  • Enable Live Tracker sharing — only when you explicitly activate and share the link

3. Data Storage

All personal data (health passport, lost & found, pilgrim progress) is stored in your device's local storage. We do not maintain a central user database. Your data stays on your device.

4. Location Tracking

Location is accessed only when you use location-dependent features. Live Tracker sharing is entirely optional and controlled by you — you decide who sees your location by sharing the unique link.

5. Pi Network Payments

All payments are processed through Pi Network's official SDK. HARAMAIN does not store wallet addresses, private keys, or payment credentials. Transaction records shown in the app are stored locally on your device only.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Al-Adhan API — anonymous prayer time requests by location
  • Open-Meteo API — anonymous weather requests by location
  • Al-Quran Cloud API — anonymous Quran text requests
  • Claude AI (Anthropic) — AI chat queries (no personal data shared)
  • Pi Network SDK — authentication and payments

7. Your Rights

You can clear all stored data at any time by using the Clear History button in Profile, or by clearing your browser/app storage. Logout removes your session from the app.

8. Contact

For privacy inquiries: support@haramain.pi · Powered by Haramain.pi

The SOS Emergency feature and emergency numbers (911, 997) are provided as quick access tools. HARAMAIN does not replace official emergency services. Always contact Saudi authorities directly in a life-threatening emergency.

The Live Tracker feature shares your GPS location via a unique link that you control. You choose who receives this link. HARAMAIN does not store or transmit your location to any server. Stop sharing by revoking the link in the Tracker settings.

6. Health Passport

Health information stored in HARAMAIN's Health Passport is stored locally on your device. HARAMAIN does not transmit medical data. The QR code generated contains only the information you entered. HARAMAIN is not a medical service.
